The CANADA List

Know what you're buying. Support what matters.

The CANADA Score utilizes AI to arrive at a single metric that estimates how "Canadian" a product or company truly is, based on publicly available information.

To calculate each score, our model evaluates a range of factors, including:

  • Whether the company is Canadian-owned
  • The extent to which it manufactures products in Canada
  • How much it sources Canadian-made components or ingredients
  • Its overall contribution to Canadian jobs and economic presence

Scores are assigned according to the following scale:

10/10 – 100% Canadian: owned, made, and sourced here.
9/10 – Canadian-owned, made here, with minimal foreign ingredients.
8/10 – Canadian-owned, mostly made here, some imports.
7/10 – Canadian-owned, but a lot of foreign sourcing.
6/10 – Foreign-owned, but made in Canada.
5/10 – Foreign-owned, about half Canadian-made.
4/10 – Foreign-owned, some Canadian manufacturing.
3/10 – Foreign-owned, Canadian-themed branding, little real contribution.
2/10 – Foreign-owned, mostly made abroad, minimal Canadian presence.
1/10 – Sold here, but zero meaningful Canadian economic involvement.
